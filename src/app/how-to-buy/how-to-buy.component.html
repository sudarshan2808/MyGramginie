Select and filter

Our Gramgenie marketplace will benefit business owner to filter through different options to find their influencer, whether it is an influencer from an artistic niche or an influencer with majority European followers, you will find it all.

Reputation and Feedback

With the help of feedback, you can view the previous works which the influencer have provided to other businesses thus bringing confidence in your purchase. The feedback will build reputation for both buyer and influencer which will enable good business practices.

Instagram account analyser

The built-in Instagram account analyser will allow business owner to search the influencer they want to work with and it will conduct a thorough analysis on followers, engagement rate, user activity, upload numbers and other useful information.

Trade confidently and securely

Gramgenie will provide you with assurance and confidence whenever you purchase a service from an influencer as we provide 100% protection for each transaction.

Pay with confidence

You can now pay with multiple vendors ranging from cryptocurrency to debit card, each payment made is secured with the use of escrow. Essentially, you will only pay once the service is delivered.
